Our Special Books

PARADOXICAL SAJID-1

Paradoxical Sajid is a series of books on Islam written by Bangladeshi writer Arif Azad. It is a collection of short stories arranged in response to various questions against the Islamic faith in the commentary of a character named Sajid. In it the author tries to disprove the anti-Islamic questions through Islamic information, logic, philosophy, science and reality and from there tries to prove the weakness of the opponent's argument.

PARADOXICAL SAJID-2

Paradoxical Sajid 2 - In the books of Paradoxical Sajid series, Sajid, in answer to the difficult question of atheists, has given proper proof of the existence of Allah by giving logical explanation in the evolution of science in the light of Qur'an. There is no God who says that only those who believe in science have shown this strange argument. With their counter-argument, Sajid explained that the creator of the world must be someone. This universe was not created by itself.

PRODUCTIVE MUSLIM

‘Productive Muslim’ is a self-improvement book. The pages of the book are full of life-long discussions on various aspects of self-awakening, self-construction and self-development. It contains the invaluable gift of the Creator — realistic strategies for harnessing our intellectual time and energy to build ourselves as a visionary and dedicated craftsman of a new world through personal formation, career development, and social work.

REVIVE YOUR HEART

How do modern Muslims maintain a spiritual connection with Allah and how do they address major challenges facing the ummah today?
